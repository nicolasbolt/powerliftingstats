Lifter Search - Details

The lifter search form checks a name, weight class and federation through the openpowerlifting data. It will show the best meet for the name entered as well as a few pie charts showing what percent of lifters have lifted more than the lifter entered and the percentage of lifters who have lifted less than the lifter entered. At the bottom, there is a link to show more details about the weight class of the lifter entered in the form. Click here for a link to the openpowerlifting database
