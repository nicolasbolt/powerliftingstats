Instructions

Customized Reports:

1. Enter the full name of a lifter in the OpenPowerlifting database (Must be the exact spelling as what is in the database)

2. Pick one of the federations available that the athlete has competed in

3. Enter the weight class of the lifter in kilograms. Enter just the number and nothing else. For SHW classes enter the highest weight class with a + (example: 120+)

General Reports:

Pick either one of the weight classes or one of the federations to generate the report

Compare Lifters:

Enter two names of two lifters in the database (Must be the exact spelling as what is in the database)

This will generate a report using the highest numbers from each lifter regardless of weight class or federation

Examples

Name: Nicolas Bolt

Federation: USAPL

Weight Class: 93

Name: Joe Sullivan

Federation: USPA

Weight Class: 100

Name: Steve Gentili

Federation: USPA

Weight Class: 140
