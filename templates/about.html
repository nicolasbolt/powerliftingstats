What Does This Site Contain?

There are different ways available to search and compare powerlifting data. The Customized Reports page searches for a specific lifter in a specific weight class and federation and returns the best meet results and compares that lifter with competitors. The Compare Lifters page will run a process that compares two lifters using absolute measures as well as relative strength measurements. Finally, the general reports page will generate reports on a specific weight class or federation. Currently, there are only a couple weight classes and federations available for that feature.

Data Provided by OpenPowerlifting

All of the powerlifting data used in the different forms on this site was provided by openpowerlifting.org.
